Base station position

For a stationary base, fixed mode is preferred: the receiver treats its antenna as immobile and broadcasts a stable ARP in RTCM 1005. Locked coordinates are saved and restored after extension restarts. They only change when you click Lock fixed position again (with new values or blank to use ARP/GGA), or when you start a survey-in (which clears the lock). Survey-in duration/accuracy setpoints are also saved; use Apply target to change them mid-survey without restarting the average.
